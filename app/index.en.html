TariffCalc

TariffCalc is an easy to use tariff calculator designed so it's simple and understandable.

Just go to the Calculator tab and fill in all the inputs with required data and you'll be returned appropriate tariff.

In case any of the fileds was not clear the Help tab containes description of each element with examples.

The FAQ tab contains answers to frequently asked questions that may help you in case the Help information wasn't enough.
